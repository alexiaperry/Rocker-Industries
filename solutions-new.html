Custom Solenoid and Solenoid Valve Solutions

Rocker Industries designs and manufactures precision solenoids and solenoid valves for aerospace, defense, and commercial applications. From custom-engineered designs to build-to-print production, we support demanding applications with responsive engineering, rapid prototyping, in-house testing, and AS9100 / ISO 9001 certified quality systems.

Two Ways We Support Your Program

Custom Design & Development

For new requirements, Rocker supports customers from specification review and concept development through analysis, prototyping, testing, and production.

Build-to-Print Manufacturing

For established designs, Rocker manufactures to customer drawings, processes, and specifications with precision, quality, and consistency.

Product Solutions

Solenoids

Rocker manufactures a wide range of solenoids for actuation needs, including:

  • Single and multiple coil solenoids
  • Pressurized and non-pressurized solenoids
  • Mechanical and ball latching solenoids
  • Current-switching solenoids
  • Rectified VAC solenoids
  • PWM solenoids

Solenoid Valves

Rocker designs and manufactures hydraulic, fuel, pneumatic, and Skydrol-compatible solenoid valves, including:

  • Two-way, three-way, and four-port designs
  • Pilot operated valves
  • Poppet and spool valves
  • Latching valve designs
  • Cartridge and inline valves
  • High-temperature applications

Built Around Your Application

Every application has its own requirements for force, stroke, pressure, voltage, flow, response time, durability, size, weight, and operating environment.

Rocker works closely with customers to match the right solenoid or solenoid valve configuration to the application.
