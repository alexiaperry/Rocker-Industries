Product Family

Solenoids

Rocker manufactures a wide range of solenoids for actuation needs, including custom and production-ready configurations for demanding applications.

  • Single & multiple coil solenoids
  • Pressurized & non-pressurized designs
  • Mechanical & ball latching solenoids
  • Current-switching, rectified VAC & PWM options
Product Family

Solenoid Valves

Rocker designs and manufactures hydraulic, fuel, pneumatic, and Skydrol-compatible solenoid valves for aerospace, defense, and commercial applications.

  • Two-way, three-way & four-port designs
  • Pilot operated, poppet & spool valves
  • Latching valve configurations
  • Cartridge, inline & high-temperature designs
