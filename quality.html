Quality

Engineered for Reliability

Quality isn’t a process — it’s our standard.

At Rocker Industries, every employee is fully aware of the human life depending upon the quality and delivery of our products. We are dedicated to world-class, error-free performance, and continuous improvement. Our quality process assures consistent conformance exceeding our internal and external customer’s expectations.
John Perry President, Rocker Industries

Rocker Industries is a Certified Dock-to-Stock supplier for the majority of our customers. We strive to find new ways to continually improve our product and processes, including implementation of a certified operator program throughout our facility.

The Quality Assurance System at Rocker Industries meets our industries’ highest requirements. Rocker Industries is certified by Det Norske Veritas (DNV) to AS9100 and ISO9001, and has certified in-house testing facilities to perform all necessary testing, including: proof pressure, flow rate, pressure drop, force, thermal, environmental, response time, life cycle and electrical check.
