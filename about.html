More Than 70 Years of Engineering Heritage

Since 1954, Rocker Industries has supported leading organizations with precision-engineered electromechanical solutions built for demanding applications.

From custom-engineered designs to build-to-print manufacturing, our team is committed to dependable products, responsive support, and long-term customer partnerships.

With decades of aerospace and defense manufacturing experience, Rocker Industries helps customers solve complex application challenges with practical engineering support and consistent execution.

Trusted by industry leaders including Airbus, Boeing, Lockheed Martin, and RTX, Rocker Industries continues to support applications where performance and reliability are critical.

Core Capabilities

Engineering Excellence

Our engineers leverage SolidWorks CAD and FEA with 2D and 3D static and motion solvers to deliver precise, application-specific designs.

Rapid Prototyping

From concept to prototype in weeks, we move quickly to help customers evaluate, refine, and validate designs without sacrificing quality.

Lean Manufacturing

Kanban and VMI capabilities designed to support supply chain efficiency and reduce lead times.

Precision Manufacturing

Decades of experience producing solenoids and solenoid valves to tight tolerances for demanding aerospace and defense applications.

In-House Testing

Comprehensive testing including proof pressure, flow rate, pressure drop, force, thermal, environmental, response time, life cycle, and electrical testing.

Quality Assurance

ISO 9001 and AS9100 certified quality systems built to meet the rigorous standards of aerospace and defense programs.
